Thermal Imaging and AI the Latest Cyber Security Threat

Photo by Pixabay: https://www.pexels.com/photo/turned-on-computer-monitor-displaying-text-270360/

Article link here November 7th, 2022

This week I am sharing an article published by CTV News that interviewed Mohammed Khamis who is an associate professor at the University of Glasgow. He was part of a team that has worked to combine thermal imaging sensors and AI to crack passwords on computers recently vacated by their operator. They tested the technique on different materials, password complexities, and even user input preferences showing an alarming high accuarcy, sometimes as high as 93% on weak passwords within 20 seconds!
